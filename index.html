About Me

After working in the advertising industry in Japan for 12 years, Aiko moved to the US to be with her loving family. She is currently a full time mom of an energetic 2 year old boy and expecting another wild one very soon. To fulfill her hunger for new learning, she currently immerses herself in coding at the Moms Can Code School with an aim to become a web developer.

During her career, Aiko has always been passionate about operational excellence and project management. She loves thinking about creative ways to work with efficiency and effectiveness. In her most recent job at Facebook, she contributed to the growth and success of the market through strong marketing consultation for her branding clients as well as empowering internal teams by building operational playbooks and strategic blueprints. Aiko is a well organized, data-driven and responsible collaborator who enjoys cross functional teamwork.

Along with her future skillset in coding, Aiko is eager to dive back into the workforce in 2020 after delivering her second son. She looks forward to be involved in product development that makes positive contribution to better education or health in the world, especially for the future of our children including her two boys!
